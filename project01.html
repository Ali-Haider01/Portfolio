About Me
Designing Is My Passion

My name is Ali Haider, and I am currently a student pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science (BSCS) at The University of Faisalabad. I am in my 5th semester and will be entering my 6th semester in January, Insha’Allah. Throughout my academic journey, I have worked on several projects, including a Quiz Game, Tic-Tac-Toe, Hospital Management System, and Pharmacy Management System using Java GUI. These projects have significantly contributed to my growth as a developer. I have acquired skills in C++, Java, HTML, CSS, TypeScript, and JavaScript, and I am currently focused on learning MERN Stack Development through online resources such as YouTube and other platforms. In addition, I am dedicating time to improving my Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) to strengthen my problem-solving and coding skills. I strive to enhance my knowledge and expertise every day. Thank you for visiting my website. Jazak Allah Khair!.
